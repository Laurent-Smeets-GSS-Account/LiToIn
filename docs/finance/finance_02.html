Variables used on this page

Variables Used in the Cost of Living Analysis Food Prices and Consumption

  • wb16/wb17: Rice quantity purchased (kg) and price paid
  • wb18/wb19: Chicken quantity purchased (kg) and price paid
  • wb20/wb21: Eggs quantity purchased and price paid

Price Perceptions and Inflation

  • vw5: Agreement with statement Prices for sembako (9 essential commodities) rose too fast over the PAST 30 DAYS
  • sv28: Subjective inflation perception (whether prices rose faster than usual, at typical pace, or slower)

Financial Resilience

  • sv30: Ability to pay emergency expense of 300,000 Rupiah using available resources

Grouping Variables

  • quint: Household wealth quintile (from poorest to richest)
  • region: Geographic region within Indonesia
  • date: Time period of observation
