Variables used on this page

Variables Used in the Borrowing and Saving Analysis Savings Methods and Behavior

  • sv3: Ability to save for the future over the past 30 days (Yes/No)
  • sv4: Amount saved in the past 30 days
  • sv5A-sv5V: Forms of savings (indicators for different methods)
    • Bank account (sv5A)
    • E-wallet (sv5B)-
    • Cash at home (sv5C)
    • Group savings (sv5D) I
    • nvestments/Other (sv5E/sv5V)

Borrowing Behavior

  • sv12: Applied for credit or loan in the past 30 days (Yes/No)
  • wb32: Subjective household economic class self-identification

Contextual Variables

  • quint: Household wealth quintile (from poorest to richest)
  • date: Time period of observation
  • infl: Inflation measure used to calculate real savings
