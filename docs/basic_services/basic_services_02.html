Percentage of Households with Water Supply Disruption in the Past 30 Days

Only data from the last 12 months is used (November 2024 to October 2025) for this analysis.

Alternative Water Sources During Disruptions

Variables used on this page

Variables Used in Water Supply Analysis Water Disruption Indicators

  • sh1: Whether household had unexpected disruption in drinking water supply in past 30 days (Yes/No)
  • sh2: Number of days with water supply disruption in past 30 days
  • sh3: Main alternative water source during disruption (categorical: piped water, well, bottled water, etc.)

Water Access and Quality Concerns

  • wat_all: Days without enough water for household needs
  • wat1: Days worried about not having enough water
  • wat2: Days clothes couldnt be washed due to water problems
  • wat3: Days with schedule changes due to water problems
  • wat4: Days with diet changes due to water problems
  • wat5: Days without handwashing after dirty activities
  • wat6: Days without body washing due to water problems
  • wat7: Days without enough drinking water
  • wat8: Frequency of anger about water situation
  • wat9: Days gone to sleep thirsty due to lack of water
  • wat10: Days with no useable/drinkable water whatsoever
  • wat11: Frequency of feeling ashamed/excluded due to water problems

Demographic and Geographic Variables

  • quint: Income quintile (1=Poorest to 5=Richest)
  • region: Geographic region within Indonesia
  • popw: Population weight for representative statistics
  • date: Time period of observation
